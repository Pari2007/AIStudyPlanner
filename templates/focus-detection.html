About Focus Detection

Understand your real focus level during study sessions by analyzing your interactions with your device.

The system detects when you switch tabs to distracting sites like Instagram and logs these events to help you improve your focus habits.

How It Works

Start a Session: Click the "Start Session" button to begin tracking your focus time.
Monitor Distractions: The system automatically detects when you switch away from the tab to check social media or other distracting websites.
Manual Logging: You can manually log distractions using the "Mark as Distracted" button if you get sidetracked.
Track Progress: View your session time and distraction count to understand your focus patterns.
Improve Over Time: Use these insights to build better study habits and increase your focus duration.

Benefits

Identify your distracting habits
Track focus consistency over time
Build accountability and awareness
Improve overall study productivity
