About Smart Timetable

Set a timetable (e.g., 1 month) and the system automatically validates if your targets are achievable within that period.

If not possible, it intelligently suggests rescheduling to keep your study goals realistic and motivating.

How It Works

Define Your Goals: Set a deadline and specify the hours needed to complete your study target.
AI Validation: The system analyzes whether your target is realistic given your available time.
Get Suggestions: If your goal isn't achievable, receive intelligent recommendations for rescheduling.
Automatic Scheduling: The AI creates an optimized study schedule based on your capacity and constraints.
Track Progress: Monitor your adherence to the timetable and adjust as needed.

Benefits

Set realistic and achievable goals
Avoid burnout with intelligent scheduling
Stay motivated with achievable targets
