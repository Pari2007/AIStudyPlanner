**Smart AI Study Planner**

Overview

The Smart AI Planner is an intelligent study management system designed to help students plan, organize, and optimize their learning process. By combining AI-driven assistance, focus monitoring, smart scheduling, and adaptive goal optimization, the platform supports students throughout their entire study journey and promotes consistent academic progress.

The system is built to be user-friendly, adaptive, and data-driven, making it suitable for students preparing for exams, managing multiple subjects, or following long-term study goals.



Key Features

1. AI Assistant (Virtual Tutor)

The AI Assistant acts as a virtual tutor that helps students at any time during their study sessions.

**Capabilities:**

* Answers questions on any topic
* Explains concepts in simple terms
* Provides guidance and study support
* Assists with problem-solving and clarification of doubts

This feature ensures that students always have access to academic help without needing external resources.

---

### 2. Focus Detection

The Focus Detection module monitors whether the user stays on the study webpage during active study sessions.

**How it works:**

* Detects tab or application switches
* Records possible distractions
* Encourages sustained focus and concentration

By tracking distractions, the system helps students become more aware of their study habits and improve focus over time.

---

---

### 4. Study Now Feature

The **Study Now** feature provides real-time recommendations on what the student should study next.

**Recommendation is based on:**

* Remaining syllabus
* Pending tasks
* Available study time

This helps students quickly decide their next study activity without confusion or delay.

---

### 5. Overall Progress Tracking

The system continuously tracks how much of the planned syllabus has been completed.

**Progress is shown as:**

* Percentage completion
* Simple visual indicators

This allows students to clearly understand their current progress compared to their target goals.

---

### 6. Smart Goal Optimization

Smart Goal Optimization dynamically adjusts study goals based on user performance.

**Adaptive behavior:**

* Increases targets if tasks are completed faster than expected
* Reduces or redistributes targets if tasks take longer
* Keeps goals realistic and achievable

This ensures sustainable study habits and prevents burnout.
